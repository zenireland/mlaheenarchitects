--- title: Shop ---

Our challenge was to bring light into a deep plan and to extend the household area. The day-lighting device
is to create ‘light towers’ which step through the plan pushing above the roof, forming a lantern-light of
glass block. The glass block presents a valuable security screen as well as diffusing the light as it enters
the shop.
The palette of materials used are granite, sandstone, white American oak, glass and stainless steel. Back to commercial

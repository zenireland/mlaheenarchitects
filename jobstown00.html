Jobstown Community College, Tallaght
Mary Laheen was the project architect in de Blacam and Meagher Architects for this 4000 s.m.
secondary school in Tallaght. The school is laid out as a series of buildings on either side
of a street. Along the street a covered way protects students from rain as they move from one
building to another. Back to education

--- title: IFSC ---

Offices for Deutschebank in Dublin’s IFSC required 2000 s.m. of office space and an
impressive Reception and Boardroom. Eileen Gray’s Lofa Sofa and Skyline rug greet the
visitor on arrival. A cherry wood reception desk is screened with hand-blown blue glass
blocks by Killian Schurmann. Cherrywood panelling encircles the Boardroom. Letters are
carved into the cherrywood and gold-leafed. Polished white limestone is the floor surface. Back to commercial

‘Landscape and Lens’ is an interpretative model of Jean Renaudie’s Apartment Complex in Ivry-sur-Seine, Paris, known as Centre Jeanne Hachette. The model was exhibited at FreeSpace, the 2018 Venice Architecture Biennale, as part of the group exhibition, Close Encounter with Remarkable Buildings.

Close Encounter will be exhibited in the Glucksman Gallery, Cork, from April 5th to June 30th and in Visual Carlow from September 22nd to 19th January 2020.

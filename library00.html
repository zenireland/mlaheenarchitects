Mary Laheen was the project architect in de Blacam and Meagher Architects for a new Library at CIT.
The reading room is a three-volume space with galleries attached. The wall material externally is a red
clay brick. The galleries are constructed of laminated beech. Reading tables are made of solid beech. Back to education

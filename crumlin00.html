--- title: Crumlin ---

Scoil Mhuire Ógh is a national school for girls between the ages of eight and twelve in Crumlin, Dublin.
The new extension is located at the street edge of the site on the busy Crumlin Road. The civic-scale
spaces immediately behind the street façade include the Library, Entrance Hall and Foyer; as well as
staff and administrative functions. The large General Purpose room, which will occasionally be used by
the community is also at this level. Five new classrooms are at upper levels of the building. Back to education

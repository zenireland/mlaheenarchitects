--- title: Cremore ---

The Cremore Clinic is a busy medical practice to the rear of a house in a residential street
in Glasnevin. The extension to the clinic stretches into the rear garden and provides
courtyard spaces to each surgery and waiting room. Filled with light, air and the constant
presence of the natural world, the building provides a pleasant working environment for
doctors and nurses and a welcoming one for clients. Back to health

--- title: Landscape and Lens - The Model ---

The model represents a landscape; earth, solid, it has mass, it has density; plants, trees, and spaces to live grow out of the landscape and emerge into the light and air at the upper reaches. Solidity and lightness are opposite characteristics but they depend on each other.

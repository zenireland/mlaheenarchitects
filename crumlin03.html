Immediately and diagonally adjacent to this arrival point is the double height Entrance
hall or Meeting space. In the everyday use of the school, this space is the centre around
which the school rotates. Held by the fair-faced concrete walls of the cross wall system,
the room looks out on the playground through the infill wall of timber panels and glass
protected by sliding and folding timber shutters. Back to education
